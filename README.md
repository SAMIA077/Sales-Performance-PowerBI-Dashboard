# Sales-Performance-PowerBI-Dashboard
5-page sales BI report: Sales Overview, Product Analysis, Customer Insights, Territory, Budget vs Actual (revenue, profit margin, budget attainment)
Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

A multi-page Power BI dashboard delivering a full view of sales performance — covering revenue, products, customers, territories, and budget tracking.

Overview

The report turns transactional sales data into an executive-ready analytics suite, with each page focused on a different business angle.

Pages


Sales Overview – Monthly revenue trend, total revenue, profit, and orders
Product Analysis – Performance by product category, subcategory, name, color, list price, and order quantity
Customer Insights – Breakdowns by occupation, yearly income, gender, marital status, and cars owned
Territory – Sales by country, region, and group
Budget vs Actual – Budget attainment %, cumulative budget vs revenue, and profit margin %


Key Measures


Total Revenue, Total Profit, Total Orders
Profit Margin %
Budget Total, Budget Attainment %
Cumulative Budget & Cumulative Revenue


Tech Stack


Power BI Desktop (.pbix)
DAX measures for revenue, profit, and budget analysis
Date hierarchy (Year → Quarter → Month → Day) for time intelligence
Custom "Aesthetic Light Blue" theme


How to Use


Install Power BI Desktop (free from Microsoft).
Open Samia_Muneer_Power_BI_Project.pbix.
Use the page tabs and slicers to explore sales, customers, territories, and budget performance.


Key Learnings


Designing a multi-page, drill-down sales report
Building budget vs actual and cumulative measures with DAX
Customer segmentation and territory-level analysis
